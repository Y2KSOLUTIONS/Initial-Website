Our Story

Fort Wayne's
Premier Shop

Kings & Cuts was founded with one goal: give Fort Wayne men a barbershop that actually takes the craft seriously. No rushed cuts, no guessing games — just clean work from people who know what they're doing.

We've served the Northside community for over a decade, building relationships one cut at a time. Whether you're a first-timer or a regular, you get the same standard of excellence.

  • Licensed and insured master barbers
  • Clean, sanitized tools — every client, every time
  • Walk-ins always welcome, no wait with appointments
  • Free parking, wheelchair accessible
